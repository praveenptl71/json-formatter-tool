# Contributor Code of Conduct

We aim to provide a welcoming, harassment-free environment.

## Our Pledge
- Be respectful and constructive
- No harassment or discrimination
- No hate speech
- No spam or irrelevant promotion

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Personal attacks
- Abusive or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information
- Disruptive behavior

## Enforcement
Violations may result in issue/PR removal or banning.

By participating, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.
